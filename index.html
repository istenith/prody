GSoC Talk

Team ISTE in collaboration with team GLUG presented the GSOC workshop with an experienced and well versed panel consisting of Shivam Kapilla @metabrainz , Yash Punia @catrobat and Abhay Raj Singh @inkspace
The panelists ran us through every step we would encounter in the way of excelling in GSOC and guided us about all the dos and don'ts for the same
The command, fluency and vast knowledge of the panelists only naturally attracted loads of attendees from all walks of student life (irrespective of their branch and year) which was certain by the downpour of likes shares and comments in the stream which were addressed gracefully by the team
We at ISTE have an aim to diversify the technical advancement of our Institute and this proved to be one of the many gateways for achieving it.

Quantum Computing

Team ISTE at NIT Hamirpur had provided the insights on Quantum Computing, which was presented by Roger Luo himself, the Creator of Quantum BFS Ecosystem and a Researcher at the University of Waterloo. His amazing insights on Quantum Computing allowed to catch a glimpse of this magical world!
From getting to know the basics of Quantum Computing to explaining the opportunities associated with it and finally running your own Quantum Circuit on a real Quantum Computer, we have got it all covered.
“Quantum computers are poised to kick-start the next computing revolution” – So we at ISTE bustling with our aim of diversifying the technical advancement of our Institute.

What is Prodyogiki?
Every technocrat’s first hand at practical implementation of what has been learnt, every nerd’s passion and the most fulfilling exploration of technology that any engineer can do. These are merely the glimpses of the gala show that "Prodyogiki 2021" will be. We at ISTE, NIT Hamirpur are ready with a chain of events designed to challenge and motivate the students at the same time, and bring out their best. Prodyogiki 2021 is filled with interactive technological sessions, workshops, events, quizzes and ample other activities for all you technocrats out there. We promise to be the melting pots of different engineering and technical fields and will set new benchmarks in taking the technological culture of NIT Hamirpur to the unprecedented marks of glory.
